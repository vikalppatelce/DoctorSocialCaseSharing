Fields on Sx Screen (Only Name and Location is compulsory):
Name: Full name of the patient
Location*: Name of the hospital or clinic
Date: Auto populated to today’s date but you can change.
Patient Id: Id to refer to locate the patient in the hospital records
Age: Age of the patient
Sex: Male/Female check box
Start Time*: of the Sx
Duration: of the Sx
Surgery Level*:
Diagnosis: Free text for patient condition
Procedure*: Depends on specialty
Ward*: General, AC, Delux etc
Emergency: Emergency/Elective check box
Surgeon/Assistant*: Team member’s name
Referred By*: Referring doctor name
Patient Type*: General, Cashless, Insurance, LIC etc
Note: Free text to enter additional details
*Fields that have memory. If you enter data once, it will remember that entry for future records so that you don’t have to enter the entire term next time.

Buttons
Save: to save the record you just entered.
Save and Main: Go back to go to Home screen after saving.
Cancel: Go back to go to Home screen without saving.
